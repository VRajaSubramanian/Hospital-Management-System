Orthopedics

Our orthopedic specialists are trained to treat conditions related to bones, muscles, and joints, including fractures, joint pain, arthritis, and spinal disorders.

Cardiology

Our expert cardiologists offer a wide range of diagnostic and treatment options for heart-related conditions such as coronary artery disease, heart attacks, arrhythmias, and heart failure.

Neurology

Our experienced neurologists provide comprehensive care for patients with neurological disorders such as Parkinson's disease, epilepsy, stroke, and multiple sclerosis.

Pediatrics

We have a dedicated team of pediatricians and child specialists who provide specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents. Our services include routine checkups, vaccinations, and treatment for common childhood illnesses.

Gynecology

Our gynecologists offer a range of services for women's reproductive health, including prenatal care, infertility treatment, and menopause management.

General Surgery

We have a team of skilled general surgeons who perform a variety of surgeries, including appendectomy, hernia repair, gallbladder removal, and more.

Emergency Medicine

We offer 24/7 emergency medical care for critical and life-threatening conditions such as heart attacks, strokes, and accidents.

Oncology

Our oncologists offer advanced cancer treatments including chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and immunotherapy. We also provide supportive care to manage the side effects of cancer treatment.
